# AuraAI – Goal & Primary User

## One-Sentence Goal

AuraAI helps people understand the true emotional tone and patterns in their conversations by analyzing chat messages and turning them into clear vibe scores, summaries, and relationship insights.

## Primary User Type

Primary user: People in romantic relationships or "situationships" who want to understand the emotional tone, red flags, and communication patterns in their chat conversations.

Everything in v1 (copy, examples, flows) should assume this as the main persona, even if others can use it later.
